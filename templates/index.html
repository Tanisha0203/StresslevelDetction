Stress Avoidance Tips

  • Exercise Regularly: Physical activity can help lower stress hormones and improve your mood.
  • Practice Deep Breathing: Deep breathing exercises can help calm your nervous system.
  • Take Breaks: Step away from stressful situations to clear your mind and regain focus.
  • Get Enough Sleep: Adequate sleep is vital for mental and physical well-being.
  • Connect with Others: Talking to a friend or family member can help alleviate stress.
  • Stay Organized: Managing tasks effectively can reduce feelings of being overwhelmed.
